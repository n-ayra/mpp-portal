Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles and objectives

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

"Menjulang tekad agenda pemerkasaan mahasiswa sebagai pemegang taruh terpenting universiti"
"Elevating the agenda of student empowerment as the most important stakeholder of the university"

Mission

"Mengangkat nilai kepimpinan dan dedikasi dalam segenap aspek
seperti pembangunan pelajar, pengurusan strategik dan integriti kewangan"
"Elevating leadership values and dedication in all aspects such as student development,
strategic management and financial integrity"

Our Strategic Objectives

These objectives guide our daily operations and long-term planning to serve UTHM students effectively.

Student Representation

Serve as the official channel between students and university administration, ensuring student voices are heard in decision-making processes.

Welfare Protection

Protect and promote student welfare and interests through advocacy, support services, and policy recommendations.

Leadership Development

Develop leadership qualities among students through training programs, workshops, and practical experiences.

Quality Programs

Organize beneficial academic, recreational, and cultural programs that contribute to holistic student development.

University Relations

Maintain positive and constructive relations with university management while advocating for student needs.

Community Engagement

Engage with the local community through service projects that benefit both students and the surrounding area.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our actions and decisions.

Transparency

We operate openly with clear communication about our activities and finances.

Inclusivity

We represent and serve all students regardless of background or affiliation.

Innovation

We continuously seek creative solutions to improve student life at UTHM.
