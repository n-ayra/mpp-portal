Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles and objectives

Vision

"To be an excellent and respected student representative body that upholds student welfare and develops future leaders."

Mission

  • Represent student voices effectively to the university administration
  • Organize programs that develop student leadership and soft skills
  • Maintain good relations between students and university management
  • Uphold student welfare and rights
  • Foster unity among students from diverse backgrounds

Our Objectives

Student Representation

Serve as the official channel between students and university administration

Welfare Protection

Protect and promote student welfare and interests

Leadership Development

Develop leadership qualities among students

Quality Programs

Organize beneficial programs for student development

University Relations

Maintain good relations with university management

Community Engagement

Engage with the local community through service projects
